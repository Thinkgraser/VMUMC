Weekly Worship

Worship Matters

J.A.M. is our Wednesday evening programming and is held through the school year. Dinner is served at 6:00 and the program goes from 6:30-7:30.

The Adult Discussion Group meets at 9:30AM in the overflow area at the back of the sanctuary. Come to church early for an interesting discussion and thoughtful study. Topics vary; a given topic may be discussed for several weeks.

Worship services are Sundays at 10:45 AM. There is a fellowship time after the service. A staffed nursery is available.

We impatiently await that which is already here.
