A Mission Moment

Support of Youth Group's Mission Work; Food Pantry in Adel

Support of S.W.A.G. (Youth Group) Mission
We are requesting donations in support of the S.W.A.G. youth group's making treats for families staying at Ronald McDonald House. They need:

oyster crackers
peanut butter
rice crispy cereal
unsalted butter
powdered sugar
melting chocolate
wax paper

Sign up here and drop off your donations in the church kitchen by Friday, February 9.

Aluminum Tabs
We are collecting the tabs from soda cans for donation to Ronald McDonald House. Aluminum tabs are easier for them to store and are higher-quality aluminum than complete cans. Of course, our church is happy to collect the whole can (or the rest of it after you remove and save the tab). Bring in your collected tops by February 10. (We'll take whole soda cans any time.)

Pancake Supper
Tuesday, February 13 (Shrove Tuesday)
5:30 - 7:00 PM
We will have a pancake supper, our traditional pancake-flipping contest, games, crafts, and fellowship. Free-will donations will go to the Perry community. Want to help? Sign up sheets are in the church entryway.

Adel (Good Samaritan) Food Pantry

Please place your donation in the marked tub in the church entryway.
